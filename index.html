Hi, I'm Belle Mason, an aspiring software developer from the picturesque county of Somerset, UK. My journey into the world of technology began at Richard Huish College, where I developed a passion for coding and problem-solving. The structured yet creative nature of software development has always fascinated me, and I'm dedicated to mastering the skills required to build innovative and efficient solutions.

Currently, I work as a supervisor at a local wine bar, where I hone my leadership and organisational skills. This role allows me to engage with a diverse group of people and manage various aspects of the business, which has been incredibly rewarding. Despite the demands of my job, I always find time to code and work on personal projects, continually expanding my knowledge and expertise in software development.

In my off-time, you can often find me immersed in coding, experimenting with new technologies, and working on exciting projects. My goal is to transition fully into a software development career, where I can leverage my skills to create impactful and innovative solutions. I'm excited about the future and the opportunities that lie ahead in the ever-evolving tech industry.
